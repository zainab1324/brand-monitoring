# Social Media Analytics for Brand Monitoring

## Overview
- Monitor and analyze brand mentions on social media to understand customer sentiment and public perception. 
- This project demonstrates techniques for extracting valuable insights from social media data.

## Objectives
- Gauge brand reputation and public perception across social platforms, 
- identify emerging issues, and 
- develop strategies for real-time brand management.

## Methodology
- Natural language processing for sentiment analysis
- Topic modeling to identify key discussion themes
- Trend analysis with time series visualization
- Social network analysis to identify influencers

## To find Insights & Learning
- Identifying key drivers of negative and positive sentiment
- Creating early-warning systems for potential PR crises
- Informing PR interventions and improving online reputation
- Measuring impact of marketing campaigns on social perception
